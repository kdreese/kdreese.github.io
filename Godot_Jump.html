Godot Jump

Game and Development Info

  • Genre:
    Platformer
  • Platform:
    PC (browser)
  • Project Status:
    Completed and released for free on Itch.io
  • Game Description:
    A space themed recreation of Doodle Jump made as a learning project in Godot. Use the springs, jetpacks, and wrapping screen to your advantage to get as high as you can while avoiding falling and the black holes along your way.
  • Role:
    Gameplay Designer, Level System Designer, Programmer
  • Tools Used:
    Godot, GitHub
  • Project Duration:
    January 2022 - March 2022
  • Team Size:
    2

Team Responsibilities and Lessons Learned

  • Designed level chunks in a wide difficulty range
  • Created a system to change the probability of level chunk generation based on player skill
  • Managed team schedule and plans for future work sessions
  • Implemented scrolling parallax background
  • Implemented background music and sound effects
  • There's a lot to be learned in replicating what works
    In recreating the gameplay and feel of an existing game, a lot of decision points led us to realizing why certain decisions in the design were made the way they were. That extra insight taught me a lot about game design, even outside of the context of the project we were working on.
  • Focusing on the goals of the project make for a more refined product
    The goal going into Godot Jump was to learn more about the Godot engine and work on specific game design practices for personal and professional improvement. With those goals at the forefront, we were able to cut down on offshoot ideas that didn't meet our original intentions. By keeping the workflow focused, we learned more of what we wanted in a shorter amount of time, and were able to keep note of the other features for research and practice at a later date.
